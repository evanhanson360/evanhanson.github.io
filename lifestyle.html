Lifestyle

Lifestyle photos are a type of photography aimed at capturing people in real-life situations, typically in a way that tells a story or evokes a specific mood.

Unlike traditional studio portraits or staged shots, lifestyle photography seeks to portray authentic moments and natural expressions, often blurring the line between documentary and commercial photography.
